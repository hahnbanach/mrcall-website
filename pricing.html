Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my minutes?

Additional minutes are billed at a competitive per-minute rate. You'll receive notifications as you approach your limit.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new accounts get a free trial period to test MrCall with your business.

Do I need special hardware?

No special hardware needed. MrCall works with your existing phone system through simple call forwarding or VoIP integration.
