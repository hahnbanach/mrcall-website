Handle tenant calls efficiently. Manage maintenance requests and inquiries without being on-call 24/7.

The problem

Property managers juggle calls from multiple buildings and tenants. Maintenance emergencies don't wait for business hours. Prospective tenants call about vacancies at all times. Without proper phone coverage, issues escalate and good tenants look elsewhere.

How MrCall helps

🔧 Maintenance requests

AI captures maintenance issues with details: location, problem description, urgency level.

🚨 Emergency routing

True emergencies (flooding, gas leaks, break-ins) are immediately forwarded to on-call staff.

🏠 Vacancy inquiries
